Hi, welcome to this simulation of Conway's Game Of Life.
The Game Of Life is a cellular automaton that consists of a regular grid of cells, each in one of the two possible states : dead or alive.
For each generation, the next states are determined following these rules :
  • A live cell with less than two live neighbors dies.
  • A live cell with more than three live neighbors dies.
  • A live cell with two or three live neighbors stays alive.
  • A dead cell with exactly three live neighbors will become a live cell.
